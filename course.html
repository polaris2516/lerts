Courses We Offer

Oxford-Based Courses

Are you looking for an excellent course that will fit your child's budding English skills? Well, we have got your back! Oxford-based courses are compilations of perfectly-knitted English lessons that teach the fundamentals of English. Letting the learners experience the English language by tackling the most basic concept to the most complex ones in the easiest way possible.

Conversation Courses

Speaking out and practicing the acquired knowledge is highly necessary to build confidence in speaking English spontaneously. In this course, dialogues and discussions are the main highlights exposing the learners to different patterns of English sentences used in an actual conversation setup. The conversation course is suitable for intermediate to advanced learners.
